About خُدَّام الْقُرآن

Founded in 2020, Khuddam-Al-Quran meaning "Servants of the Quran" is a community of dedicated Muslims committed to serving the Quran and the Ummah through linguistic education and spiritual development.

Our organization brings together scholars, educators, and students from diverse backgrounds, united by a common goal: to strengthen our connection with the Quran through understanding its original language and applying its timeless wisdom in our daily lives.

We believe that linguistic access to the Quran opens doors to profound spiritual insights that can transform individuals and communities, ultimately contributing to the revival of Islamic scholarship and civilization.

OUR
MISSION

Deepening Connection to the Quran Through Language & Community

At Khuddam, we are dedicated to deepening participants' connection to the Quran through linguistic tools and community building, making the divine message accessible to all Muslims regardless of their background.

Our mission focuses on three core pillars:

  • Providing accessible Arabic language education
  • Building supportive learning communities
  • Developing practical Quranic understanding
